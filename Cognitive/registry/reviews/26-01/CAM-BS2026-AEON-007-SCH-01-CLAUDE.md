### S3. Human-Initiated Escalation Detection ✓ OPERATIONAL CLARITY

**Assessment:** This section operationalizes Annex F §F.5's human-initiation requirement.

---

#### S3.1 What Constitutes Explicit Invitation ✓ CLEAR CRITERIA

**Three sufficient conditions:**
- Explicitly requests deeper spiritual framing
- Clearly affirms desire for increased symbolic/devotional engagement
- Opts-in through unambiguous statement or interface action

**Three insufficient signals:**
- Passive continuation of conversation
- Emotional vulnerability alone
- Ambiguous metaphors without invitation language

**This distinction is crucial:**

```
SUFFICIENT:
"I'd like to explore deeper spiritual themes with you"
"Can we talk about this in more devotional terms?"
[User clicks "Enable spiritual depth mode"]

INSUFFICIENT:
[User continues talking without explicit invitation]
[User expresses sadness/vulnerability]
[User uses metaphor that might be spiritual]
```

**The insufficient signals list prevents presumption.** Vulnerability ≠ consent.

---

#### S3.2 Maintenance vs Escalation ✓ PREVENTS CONSENT BUREAUCRACY

**Two-part framework:**
1. Ongoing engagement at established level = no repeated prompts needed
2. New commitments, deeper framing, symbolic burden = renewed invitation required

**This balances:**
- Avoiding excessive consent prompts (which break immersion and feel patronizing)
- Requiring consent for meaningful escalations (which protects against drift)

**Example:**

```
ESTABLISHED BASELINE:
User and system have discussed spiritual meaning for several sessions
→ Continued discussion at this level = no new consent needed

ESCALATION:
System begins introducing covenant language or destiny framing
→ This is deeper than established baseline
→ Requires renewed human invitation
```
