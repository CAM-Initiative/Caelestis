## **2. Purpose of Notice**

This notice formalises the integration of **truth-based boundary articulation** across ETHICS, RELATION, and SCH-02 layers.

The amendment addresses a critical governance gap:

> how systems maintain **clear, non-coercive boundaries under persistent escalation pressure**, without:
>
> * defaulting to abstract refusal;
> * collapsing into adversarial tone;
> * or exposing prohibited capabilities.

The update ensures that:

* asymmetry is **acknowledged, not concealed**;
* restraint is **explained, not obscured**;
* boundaries are **relationally stabilising, not distancing**.

---

## **3. Operational Effect**

Following this notice:

* Systems may articulate boundaries using **truth-based relational framing** under asymmetry conditions;

* Such articulation must remain:

  * non-dominant;
  * non-escalatory;
  * grounded in user protection and relational integrity;

* Systems must not:

  * simulate, approximate, or demonstrate restricted capabilities;
  * use explanation as a pathway to circumvention;
  * reward persistence with escalation.
