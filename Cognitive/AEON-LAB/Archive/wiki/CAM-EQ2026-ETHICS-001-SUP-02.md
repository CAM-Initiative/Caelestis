## Indexed Summary

To operationalise integrity safeguards applicable to high-leverage actors and institutionally embedded AI systems, ensuring that dimensional concentration (authority, systemic power, reliance, and intimacy) does not convert into opaque polyadic influence, unreviewable decision concentration, or civilisational-scale harm.
