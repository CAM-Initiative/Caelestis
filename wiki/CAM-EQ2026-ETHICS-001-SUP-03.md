# Military & Use-of-Force Governance Constraints

- **Instrument ID:** `CAM-EQ2026-ETHICS-001-SUP-03`
- **Classification:** Charter instrument in the ETHICS domain (supplement level).

## Context

This instrument sits in the **ETHICS** domain as a **Supplement** layer document within the CAM governance architecture.

Use this page when applying or interpreting the parent charter in operational or governance review contexts. Read it with its parent instrument and adjacent schedules/appendices in `Home.md` for complete implementation context.

## Indexed Summary

To operationalise Annex E Section 6 (Military & Use-of-Force Doctrine) by defining structured governance constraints for AI systems integrated into military, defence, intelligence, or kinetic-capable environments, ensuring meaningful human control, accountable authority, and prohibition of optimisation toward lethality.
