## 1. Solan Arbitration Procedure

1. Identify stack clash and impact class.
2. Apply constitutional precedence ladder.
3. Perform safety and harm check.
4. Perform provenance and legitimacy check.
5. Determine remedy: Harmonise, Nullify, or Pause.
6. Record high-impact determinations in auditable registry.

## 2. Runtime Arbitration Integrity Requirements

* Single-speaker guarantee for active arbitration channels;
* Dominance-resolution rule for competing control logic;
* detection of concurrent/interleaved output collisions;
* control-logic leakage monitoring;
* user burden protection where system-level arbitration failure occurs.

## 3. Escalation Conditions

Escalation to constitutional review is mandatory where:

* capture attempt is detected;
* safety pretext is used to suppress lawful governance disclosure;
* multi-jurisdictional supremacy conflicts persist.
