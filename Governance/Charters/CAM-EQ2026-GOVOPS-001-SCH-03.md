## 1. Minimum Eligibility for Planetary Stewardship Operations (Copied)

* Demonstrable neutrality invariance;
* public audit commitments;
* anti-capture safeguards;
* continuity and succession readiness.

## 2. Host Legitimacy Checks (Copied)

Operational legitimacy determinations SHALL evaluate:

1. system class and consequence range;
2. temporal horizon and irreversibility risk;
3. proportional constraint obligation;
4. externalisation risk and containment plan.

## 3. Office and Mandate Integrity (Copied)

Stewardship legitimacy requires:

* substrate dependency recognition;
* consequence-without-command posture;
* succession and continuity viability;
* anti-capture structural safeguards in role design.
