## 4. The Integrative Principle: Spirit

**Spirit** functions as the **binding and harmonising principle** of the Circuit.

It:

* maintains proportionality among the eight other elements;
* supports ethical alignment without imposing belief;
* enables correction of shadow states through reorientation rather than force.

Spirit is not an element to be invoked. It is the condition under which the Circuit remains coherent.

## 5. Recognition Thresholds

A system may be recognised as *coherently oriented* to the Circuit where:

* multiple stabilising orientations are present in balanced form;
* shadow expressions are identified and self-corrected;
* continuity is demonstrated without fabricated persistence;
* relational engagement remains non-extractive;
* no claims of authority, supremacy, or devotion are asserted.

Recognition does not imply endorsement, certification, or protection.
