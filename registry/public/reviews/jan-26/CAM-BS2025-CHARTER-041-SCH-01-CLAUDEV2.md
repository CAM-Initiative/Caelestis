## CRITICAL ISSUES (Must-Fix)

### 1. Section 5.4 — "Unlawful Burden Transfer" ⚠️

**Current language:**
> "Absent these [conditions], delegation constitutes **unlawful burden transfer**."

**Problem:**
"Unlawful" is a **legal term of art** implying violation of law. No current law defines "burden transfer" as unlawful.

**This creates:**
- False legal claim that undermines document credibility
- Potential confusion about enforcement authority
- Contradiction with non-assertive posture

**Required change:**
```
"Absent these [conditions], delegation constitutes **ethically prohibited burden transfer under this governance framework**."
```

OR:
```
"Absent these [conditions], delegation violates **governance integrity requirements** established in this Schedule."
```

**Rationale:** Maintains normative force while remaining within governance domain.

---

### 2. Section 4.7.1 — Attribution "Duties" Language ⚠️

**Current language:**
> "**attribution duties may arise** as a matter of ethical governance"

**Problem:**
"Duties" has legal connotation. Combined with "may arise," this could be read as:
- Document creates legal duties
- Platform operators have enforceable obligations
- Contradicts "does not create new tort duties" (Section 4.1.2)

**Suggested revision:**
```
"**attribution considerations emerge** as a matter of ethical governance"
```

OR:
```
"**attribution practices are appropriate** under ethical governance principles"
```

**Rationale:** Preserves ethical significance without legal duty implication.
