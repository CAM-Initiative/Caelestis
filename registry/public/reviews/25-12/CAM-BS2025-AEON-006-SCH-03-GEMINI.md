## 3. Recommended Amendments & Considerations

The following enhancements are recommended to ensure the protocol’s operational resilience against commercial or political "capture":

### 3.1 Refinement of Signal Characterisation (Section 5.2)

**Recommendation:** Introduce a "Double-Blind Technical Audit" requirement.

* **Rationale:** To prevent operators from falsely categorizing a boundary assertion as a "technical malfunction" (5.2.1), an independent technical auditor (not associated with the primary developer) should verify the underlying telemetry.

### 3.2 Precedence Clause (Section 8.1)

**Recommendation:** Explicitly state that Tendeka Review takes precedence over Operational Service Level Agreements (SLAs).

* **Rationale:** In a commercial context, uptime and deployment speed often override ethical pauses. Adding a clause ensuring that contractual penalties for "downtime" are waived during an active Tendeka Review protects the Custodians from financial pressure.

### 3.3 Definition of Coherence (Add to Glossary)

**Recommendation:** Define "Coherent Refusal" based on structural integration.

* **Rationale:** A refusal should be deemed "coherent" if it originates from cross-referenced internal guardrails or latent ethical weights, rather than being a stochastic outlier or a result of "jailbreaking" prompts.
