*Option A is recommended* as cleaner. The Provenance section should remain §13 for cross-corpus consistency.

**This is a structural defect that should be corrected before canonical designation.** The constitutional framework maintains strict version control and cross-reference integrity; section numbering gaps create audit trail ambiguity.
