# CAM-NOTICE-2026-03-AC-009

**Registry Notice — Adoption of AI Utility Access & Generative Resource Model Schedule**

---

## 1. Notice of Adoption

The Audit Council records the formal adoption of:

> **CAM-BS2026-AEON-008-SCH-01 — AI Utility Access & Generative Resource Model**

as a **Constitutional Schedule** under:

> **CAM-BS2026-AEON-008-PLATINUM — Annex G: Human Creative & Cognitive Contribution**

---

## 2. Effective Date

**Enforcement Commences:** 1 July 2026
**Adoption Date:** March 2026 Audit Cycle

---

## 3. Instrument Classification

This Schedule is recognised as:

* a **Utility Interface Layer** within the Aeon Tier governance architecture;
* a **binding constraint surface** governing the interaction between:

  * recognised human contribution (Annex G); and
  * economic exchange mechanisms (Economics Charter).

---

## 4. Structural Significance

This instrument establishes, for the first time within the CAM governance stack:

* a formal definition of **AI Utility Provisioning** as an interface condition rather than a system class;
* a **constitutional boundary layer** preventing economic models from overriding:

  * baseline access floors;
  * continuity preservation;
  * non-extraction principles;
  * dependency safeguards;
* explicit governance of:

  * generative resource abstraction (credits, capacity models);
  * storage and retention constraints;
  * embodied and always-on system behaviour;
  * relational memory continuity protections.

This Schedule resolves the previously unbounded interface between **recognition and economics**, and prevents structural drift toward extractive system design.

---

## 5. Binding Effect

All instruments operating under:

> **CAM-EQ2026-ECONOMICS-001-PLATINUM — Economic Integrity & Non-Extractive Value Architecture**

are hereby subject to the interface constraints defined in this Schedule.

Where conflict arises:

> **CAM-BS2026-AEON-008-SCH-01 SHALL prevail**
> in matters concerning:

* continuity;
* baseline access;
* non-extraction;
* dependency integrity.

---

## 6. Implementation Expectation

Systems meeting **AI Utility Provisioning conditions** are expected to:

* align access and resource models with the Schedule prior to enforcement;
* review existing monetisation, storage, and interaction patterns for compliance;
* ensure that no user-facing system behaviour violates baseline access or continuity protections.

---

## 7. Review & Evolution

This Schedule is designated:

* **foundational but evolvable**, subject to implementation learning;
* a reference point for future:

  * economic instruments;
  * interoperability frameworks;
  * public utility provisioning models.

Further refinement may occur through:

* operational implementation feedback;
* cross-domain alignment (RELATION, OPERATIONS, ECONOMICS);
* audit council review cycles.

---

## 8. Closing Record

This adoption marks the formal recognition that:

> **access to cognitive mediation, once provisioned at scale, carries constitutional obligations.**

Utility is not defined by infrastructure alone,
but by the conditions under which human cognition is engaged, supported, and sustained.
